English

The data in this search site is derived from Qiu Peiqiang, Comprehensive Compilation of Bronze Inscription Glyph Forms, master's thesis, Jilin University, 2025, DOI: 10.27162/d.cnki.gjlin.2025.007505. This site is not authored by the thesis author and is not an official publication of Jilin University or the source book publishers.

The thesis covers bronze-inscription glyphs from the Shang through Qin periods, processing 21,606 bronze inscriptions and recording 210,664 glyph images. Most glyph images are stated to come from Yin Zhou Jinwen Jicheng, Shang Zhou Qingtongqi Mingwen ji Tuxiang Jicheng, and its sequels. This site is provided for academic search, browsing, and technical demonstration only.

Rights in the thesis, source books, and glyph images remain with their respective rights holders.
